Accessibility Statement

Paw Place is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive and user-friendly experience on our website, [insert website URL], and are continuously working to improve its accessibility.

Accessibility Features:
Our website is designed to be compatible with screen readers and other assistive technologies. We use semantic HTML to ensure that content is structured and navigable. Text alternatives are provided for non-text content, such as images and videos. We aim to maintain a high level of color contrast to enhance readability. Keyboard navigation is supported throughout the site to assist users who cannot use a mouse. Ongoing Efforts: We are actively working to improve the accessibility of our website and are committed to following the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1. We regularly review our content and design to ensure compliance with these standards.
